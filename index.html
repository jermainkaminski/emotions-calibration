Emotions Pre-Calibration

Explanation

This pre-calibration draws upon a randomly sampled audioclip set from the TESS (T)[1], RAVDESS (R)[2], SAVEE (S)[3] and CREMA-D (C)[4] audio datasets. These datasets have been documented to show relatively high IRR in intercoder tasks. The audio clips below only serve to pre-calibrate respondents in any emotion-tagging task. Every coder is recommended to listen once to all of the samples below.

1. Angry

2. Disgust

3. Fear

4. Happy

5. Sad

6. Surprise

7. Neutral
